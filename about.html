About SLC Feed

Dedicated to serving your aviary with excellence

Our Mission

SLC Feed is committed to providing reliable, high-quality bird feed delivery to aviaries, sanctuaries, and bird enthusiasts throughout the Salt Lake City area. We understand that caring for birds requires consistency and trust, which is why we've built our business on three core values: Reliability, Availability, and Dependability.

Why We Do It

We believe that birds deserve the best care possible. A significant part of that care is providing consistent, nutritious feed delivered on schedule. We've seen too many facilities struggle with inconsistent supply chains, which is why we started SLC Feed. We wanted to create a business that puts your birds' needs first—and never lets you down.

Our Commitment

🐦 Quality Products

We source only the finest bird feed, carefully selected for nutritional value and freshness.

📅 Reliable Scheduling

Your delivery schedule is sacred to us. We plan around your needs and stick to our commitments.

💬 Open Communication

We're always available to discuss your aviary's needs, answer questions, and provide support.

🌱 Continuous Improvement

We regularly evaluate and improve our service to meet the evolving needs of our customers.

The SLC Feed Difference

Unlike larger, impersonal feed suppliers, we treat each customer as a valued partner. We take pride in understanding your specific needs—whether you're running a large aviary, a bird sanctuary, or maintain a special collection. We're flexible, responsive, and genuinely invested in your success.

When you work with SLC Feed, you're not just getting a supplier—you're getting a partner committed to the health and happiness of your birds.
